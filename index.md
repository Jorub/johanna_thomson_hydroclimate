## Research profile

My work sits at the intersection of **hydrology, climate data analysis, and environmental modelling**. I evaluate large-scale observational and model-based datasets, with particular attention to evapotranspiration, precipitation, uncertainty, and hydroclimatic change.

I develop reproducible workflows for comparing datasets across space and time, diagnosing disagreement among products, and communicating the consequences of methodological choices. My research combines environmental process knowledge with statistical analysis, data visualization, and open scientific software.

## Current directions

- Multi-source assessment of terrestrial evapotranspiration trends
- Methods for interpreting agreement and disagreement among Earth observation datasets
- Reproducible environmental data infrastructure and scientific software
- Uncertainty-aware analysis for hydrology and Earth system research
