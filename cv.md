# Curriculum vitae

## Profile

Environmental scientist specializing in hydrology, hydroclimate data analysis, environmental modelling, and reproducible scientific computing. My research spans large-scale climate and hydrological datasets, terrestrial water-cycle dynamics, and uncertainty arising from dataset and method selection.

## Research appointments

### Postdoctoral Researcher

**Czech University of Life Sciences Prague** · Prague, Czechia  
*April 2022–present*

Postdoctoral research within the **Investigation of the Terrestrial Hydrological Cycle Acceleration (ITHACA)** project. My work focuses on large-scale hydroclimatic dataset analysis, terrestrial water-cycle dynamics, and reproducible scientific workflows.

- Develop reproducible pipelines for harmonizing and analysing global climate and hydrological datasets using R, NetCDF, CDO, and Bash
- Evaluate reanalysis, model, and satellite products, with spatial aggregation across climate zones, vegetation classes, elevation ranges, and IPCC regions
- Analyse hydroclimatic trends, ensemble uncertainty, and inter-dataset differences
- Produce geospatial visualizations and publish reproducible datasets and workflows through GitHub and Zenodo
- Contribute to open-source R packages and coordinate collaborative workflows, workshops, and scientific outputs

### Research Assistant

**Czech University of Life Sciences Prague** · Prague, Czechia · Part-time  
*January 2020–March 2022*

Research across large-scale geospatial analysis, hydrological systems, and environmental machine learning.

- Developed PostgreSQL/PostGIS workflows for large DEM and HydroSHEDS datasets
- Derived geomorphological metrics and hierarchical catchment analyses using Pfafstetter identifiers
- Integrated hydrological parameters with climate, vegetation, and lithological datasets
- Applied Random Forest, support vector machines, partial least squares, and ensemble methods to soil carbon and arsenic prediction from NIR–VIS spectra
- Analysed preferential-flow pathways and classified rain-on-snow and avalanche-related processes

### Researcher

**T. G. Masaryk Water Research Institute** · Prague, Czechia · Part-time  
*January 2018–December 2019*

Contributed to applied hydrology and water-management projects concerning heavy-rainfall risk, environmental policy, and minimum residual flows.

- Coordinated international activities and standardized data collection for the INTERREG RAINMAN project
- Helped prepare a transnational catalogue of heavy-rainfall mitigation measures
- Produced hydrological analyses, visualizations, reports, and technical briefing materials for public authorities

### Research Assistant

**University of Otago** · Dunedin, New Zealand  
*November 2014–August 2015*

Supported experimental freshwater ecology and aquatic sediment research, including stream mesocosm experiments, macroinvertebrate identification, sequential phosphorus extraction, preparation for ICP analysis, and principal component analysis in R.

### Weltwärts Volunteer

**Integrated Weaker Sections of Rural India** · Semiliguda, Odisha, India  
*September 2009–August 2010*

Supported reporting and village visits for a rural-development NGO working on micro-hydro power and small-scale infrastructure in rural Odisha.
