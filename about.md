# About

Johanna R. Thomson is an environmental scientist working across hydrology, climate data analysis, environmental modelling, and reproducible research.

Her current work focuses on evaluating large-scale climate and hydrological datasets, assessing uncertainty in terrestrial water-cycle estimates, and building open workflows for environmental data analysis.
